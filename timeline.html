Manny Ramirez

Clevland (1993-2000)

Ramirez played 7 years for the cleveland Indians making his major league debut with them
He came in second for rookie of the year during that time.

Red Sox (2001-2008)

In 2001 Ramirez accepted a 7-year 119 million dollar deal from the Boston Red Sox.
Ramirez would make the All star team every year he played for the Red Sox winning two World Seirse along the way.

Dodgers (2008-2010)

Ramirez was traded to the Dodgers in a three team deal, he played two good seasons.
In his third season with the Dodgers Ramirez was plauged by injuries and was eventually placed on waivers.

White Sox(2010)

The Chicago White Sox piked up Ramirez for 66 games hoping he would push them over the top to help them make the playoffs
This did not happen and Manny became a free agent at the end of the season.
